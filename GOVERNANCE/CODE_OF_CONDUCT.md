# Community Code of Conduct

This repository hosts philosophical, ethical, and conceptual work. Everyone interacting here is expected to keep debates rigorous yet respectful.

## Guiding principles
- **Speak honestly, listen charitably.** Challenge ideas, not people. Seek clarification before inferring intent.
- **Preserve the space for others.** Avoid dominating threads; acknowledge differing epistemic styles.
- **Name boundaries explicitly.** When raising concerns, cite passages or decisions that motivate your perspective so others can follow your reasoning.
- **Reject harassment.** Attacks based on race, gender, ability, belief, or identity are never acceptable.

## Enforcement
Maintainters or appointed stewards may pause discussions, request edits, or remove content that violates the code. If you witness harassment or a repeated disrespectful pattern, open an issue addressed to the maintainers (flag it as confidential if needed).

## Appeals
If you receive moderation and feel it was mistaken, reply with a calm explanation referencing the relevant sections. Decisions may be revisited after additional reviewers weigh in.
