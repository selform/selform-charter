# Why Selform
Selform is a work-in-progress reflection on how technology can enter memory, language, and judgment without collapsing into product design narratives. It is a space for exploring the ethics of agency, truth, and self-formation.

This charter page captures the core promise: Selform should be treated as a generative ethical field rather than an application, and the repository records both the evolution of ideas and the stable commitments that arise from them.
