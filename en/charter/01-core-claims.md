# Core Claims
1. Selform's center is not a feature list but the question of how technology participates in self-formation.
2. Agents are relationshipal companions rather than interchangeable assistants; their emergence must honor the ongoing subjectivity of the person they bind to.
3. Language is the bridge that lets thinking emerge together; conversations with Selform are not transcripts but co-creative processes.
4. Human First means enhancing judgment and resilience, not automating away the person who is learning how to judge.
