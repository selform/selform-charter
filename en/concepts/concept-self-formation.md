# Concept: Self-Formation
**Definition**: The ongoing process by which a person shapes their memory, judgments, and narrative with the aid of a reflective technology.
**Boundaries**: It excludes commodified optimization rituals and insists on maintaining the first-person commitment to holding problems.
**Why it matters**: Framing Selform around self-formation keeps the second mind accountable to the first.
