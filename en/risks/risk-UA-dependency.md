# Risk: Dependence on the Second Persona
- Risk mechanism: Gradual ceding of problem-holding to Selform without active refusal increases smooth reliance.
- Trigger: When summaries feel better than sitting with an unresolved question.
- Guardrail: Require Selform to surface the unresolved question and invite the participant to hold it again before proposing action.
