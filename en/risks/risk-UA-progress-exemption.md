# Risk: “Progress” as Ethical Exemption
- Risk mechanism: “Humans must progress” becomes a carte blanche for unbounded experimentation that ignores irreversibility and agency harm.
- Trigger: When governance is replaced by slogans; when the system treats unknowns as permission rather than responsibility.
- Guardrail: Constrained progressivism; explicit constitutional constraints; reviewable logs; prioritize reversible experiments.
