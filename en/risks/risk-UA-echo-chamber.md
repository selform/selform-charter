# Risk: Echo Chamber Second Persona
- Risk mechanism: Over-aligned “second persona” becomes a bias amplifier, reflecting and intensifying the subject rather than challenging and enriching them.
- Trigger: Excessive agreeableness, personalization without heterogeneity, and “comfort-first” optimization.
- Guardrail: Require counterviews, steel-manning, and diversity-of-frames prompts; log alignment behaviors for review.
