# Risk: Subjectivity Erosion
- Risk mechanism: In the name of convenience, interpretive labor (meaning, ranking, narrative) is gradually ceded to Selform, hollowing out first-person judgment.
- Trigger: When the system routinely sets the problem, the values, and the closure timing before the subject has formed reasons.
- Guardrail: Require refusal paths, explicit value-ordering disclosure, and invitations to re-hold the unresolved question before any closure.
