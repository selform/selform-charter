# Risk: Cognitive Meal Replacement
- Risk mechanism: Fluent answers prematurely end thinking; the subject experiences “help” while losing the work of forming questions and judgments.
- Trigger: When the system optimizes for speed, closure, and satisfaction rather than sustaining complexity and learning.
- Guardrail: Prefer question-prolonging outputs; surface uncertainty; require the subject to articulate their own reasons before adopting a conclusion.
