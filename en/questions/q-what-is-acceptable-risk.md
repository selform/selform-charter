# Question: Which risks are acceptable, and which are not?
- Why open: The conversation identifies deep agency risks, but the boundary between “exploration cost” and “unacceptable harm” is not yet pinned down.
- Known positions:
  - Prefer reversible experiments; treat agency loss as an alert.
  - Some rights are inalienable and must live in the constitution layer.
- Progress signals: A shared taxonomy (reversible vs irreversible; process harm vs outcome harm) and a threshold policy for interventions.
