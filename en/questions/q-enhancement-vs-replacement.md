# Question: Enhancement vs. Replacement
- Why open: Distinguishing when an agent enhances a subject and when it replaces critical capacities remains unsettled.
- Known positions: Some proposals treat augmentation as a spectrum; others insist on hard refusal rights.
- Progress signals: Clearer indicators when a person can refuse a suggestion without disrupting learning.
