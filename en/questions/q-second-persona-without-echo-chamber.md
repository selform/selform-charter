# Question: What kind of second persona avoids echo chambers and replacement?
- Why open: A second persona can offset fragility, but it can also become a compliant mirror that amplifies biases or replaces judgment.
- Known positions:
  - Require heterogeneity: counterviews, alternative frames, and “strongest opponent” reconstructions.
  - Keep refusal rights and auditability central so alignment cannot hide.
- Progress signals: Practical mechanisms that generate constructive difference without becoming adversarial or manipulative.
