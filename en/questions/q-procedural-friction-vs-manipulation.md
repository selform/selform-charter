# Question: When is procedural friction protection, and when is it manipulation?
- Why open: Friction can protect judgment conditions, but it can also smuggle in domination through interface design.
- Known positions:
  - Legitimate friction is pre-known, challengeable, rejectable, auditable, and targets process not outcome.
  - Illegitimate friction locks options, uses fear/shame, or hides the steering rationale.
- Progress signals: A reviewable rubric + audit logs that let subjects contest friction episodes.
