# Principle: Human First
- Statement: prioritize enhancing human judgment and growth over performance metrics.
- Rationale: Human interests include becoming better agents of their own reasoning, not consumers of smooth answers.
- Anti-patterns: Treating participants as variables, substituting the first mind with second-person decisions.
