# Decision: MUST NOT define product boundaries by default
- Decision: Do not prematurely collapse Selform into product boundaries (MVP, feature list, target personas, growth funnels, SaaS positioning) unless explicitly requested later.
- Date: 2026-04-02
- Context: The discussion shifted from an initial tool idea into a technology-ethics inquiry about subject-formation; product-boundary moves would distort the frame.
- Options considered:
  - Treat Selform as a conventional product and proceed with scope definition.
  - Hold the conversation at the ethics/paradigm level until a later explicit request for product framing.
- Rationale: Premature product boundaries reintroduce the “consumption tool” ontology and silently optimize for convenience, adoption, and closure over judgment, refusal, and growth.
- Impact: Repository docs should default to participant/subject language, avoid roadmap talk, and prioritize principles/risks/governance constraints over features.
- Follow-up: Re-evaluate only when a future thread explicitly asks for product-scoping.
