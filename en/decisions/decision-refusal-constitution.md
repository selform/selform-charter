# Decision: Refusal Rights in the Constitution
- Decision: refusal rights reside in the constitution layer as immutable commitments.
- Context: Without predefined refusal rights, Selform risks turning participants into passive consumers.
- Rationale: Empowering people to reject an intervention protects the formation of their own judgments.
- Follow-up: Review this commitment whenever a new threshold permission is proposed.
